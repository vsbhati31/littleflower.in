About Little Flower Preschool

Where curiosity blossoms, creativity thrives, and every child blooms into their best self.

Our Story

Little Flower Preschool was founded with a heartfelt mission — to create a place where children are not just educated, but celebrated for who they are. From our humble beginnings to becoming one of the most trusted preschools in Silvassa, our journey has always been rooted in love, care, and excellence.

We understand that the early years are the most critical in a child’s life. That’s why we’ve built a nurturing space where little learners feel safe to explore, confident to ask questions, and inspired to dream big. Every corner of our school is thoughtfully designed to stimulate curiosity, encourage creativity, and cultivate a lifelong love for learning.

Our Philosophy

At Little Flower Preschool, we believe education should be joyful, meaningful, and holistic. Our approach blends play-based learning with structured activities, allowing children to develop essential life skills while having fun. We foster a positive environment where respect, kindness, and empathy are as important as reading, writing, and numbers.

  • Learning through exploration and discovery
  • Building confidence through encouragement
  • Instilling values of respect, empathy, and teamwork
  • Balancing academics with creativity and play

Why Families Choose Little Flower Preschool

  • Safe, child-focused environment with CCTV security
  • Experienced, passionate, and caring educators
  • Vibrant classrooms designed to inspire learning
  • Engaging curriculum tailored to each child’s needs
  • Strong parent-school partnerships

Our Programmes

  • Playgroup (2–3 years) — Gentle introduction to school with fun, hands-on activities.
  • Nursery (3–4 years) — Building social skills, early literacy, and numeracy through play.
  • Junior KG (4–5 years) — Strengthening academic foundations and creative thinking.
  • Senior KG (5–6 years) — Preparing for primary school with confidence and curiosity.

World-Class Standards, Local Care

While we take pride in offering global best practices in early childhood education, we remain deeply connected to our community. Our teaching methods draw from internationally recognized standards while embracing the culture, traditions, and values of our local families.
