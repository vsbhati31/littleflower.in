Our Story

Little Flower Preschool was founded by four passionate educators who wanted a joyful, safe, and academically sound preschool in the heart of Silvassa. Drawing on decades of experience, we created a place where each child is known, loved, and encouraged to explore.

Our Philosophy

We believe early learning is best when it is playful, purposeful, and supported by caring adults. Our approach blends CBSE-aligned early years principles with play-based activities — fostering language, curiosity, motor skills, and social confidence.

Our Values

  • Nurturing: Small groups, warm caregivers, and individualized attention.
  • Safety: Secure pickup, trained staff, and CCTV-monitored campus.
  • Expertise: Founder-led classrooms and continuous teacher development.
  • Community: Strong parent partnerships and local festival celebrations.
  • Growth: Holistic focus — emotional, social and early academic skills.
