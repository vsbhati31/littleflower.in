Our Programmes

Tailored learning experiences for every age group

Playgroup (2-3 years)

A fun, safe space for toddlers to explore, play, and start building social skills. Focus on sensory play, language exposure, and motor skills.

Nursery (3-4 years)

Structured learning through play with a focus on early literacy, numeracy, and creative expression. Encouraging independence and curiosity.

Kindergarten (4-5 years)

Preparation for formal schooling with balanced academics, creative activities, and social-emotional learning based on CBSE pattern.

Special Activities

Art & Craft

Boosting creativity with painting, clay modeling, and craft projects.

Music & Dance

Developing rhythm, coordination, and self-expression through music and movement.

Outdoor Play

Promoting physical health, teamwork, and fun in our safe outdoor environment.
