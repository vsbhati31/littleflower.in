Our Programmes

Designed for every stage of your child’s early years

Programmes Offered

  • Playgroup (1.5 – 2.5 years) First steps into group learning with music, movement, and sensory activities.
  • Nursery (2.5 – 3.5 years) Early literacy, numeracy, art and outdoor play to build confidence and skills.
  • Junior KG (3.5 – 4.5 years) Exploring language, numbers, science, stories, and teamwork.
  • Senior KG (4.5 – 5.5 years) Ready for primary school with reading, writing, math, and real-world projects.
